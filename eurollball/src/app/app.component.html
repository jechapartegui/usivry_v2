About the Tournaments 

Our tournaments are the first of their kind in Europe. Both women and men compete for glory over an exciting weekend.

Event Dates 

Join us on February 11th and 12th, 2024, for an action-packed weekend.

Location 

We'll be at the Venise Gosnat Complex: 30 rue Amedée Huon, 94200 Ivry-sur-Seine. Our venue includes a gymnasium and a sports hall for the games.

Categories 

We have two categories: Women's Tournament (minimum age: 14) and Men's Tournament (minimum age: 16).

Registration Fees 

For foreign teams: €50, For French teams: €100.

Extras 

We'll assist with accommodation and transportation to Ivry-sur-Seine. We can't wait to compete with you.
